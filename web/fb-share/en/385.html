An integral plan for transport and communication infrastructures

We will implement an integral plan for the improvement of transport and communication infrastructures in the Valencia Region. This will:

  • Bring to an end the toll charge on the AP-7 road, and improve its access
  • Improve regional and local rail networks
  • Improve transport infrastructures in the central area of the Valencia Region, and its connections with the rest of Spain, Europe and the Mediterranean.
  • Revitalize the sea ports of Alicante and Castellón, building rail connections as a priority.
