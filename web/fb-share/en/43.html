Introducing flexibility and autonomy in teaching and research

We will promote improvements in the evaluation system of teaching and research, to ensure innovation and autonomy at all levels in research and education.

We will change the old pyramidal hierarchy structure where contracts are given (and terminated) by the State, and move instead towards a meritocratic governance model where those responsible for managing the contracts are held accountable for their results before Parliament, the group of people under contract, and before society at large.
