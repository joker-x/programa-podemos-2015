Urgent re-industrialisation plan for La Rioja

As part of the investment for Europe plan (also known as the "Juncker Plan"), we will include the La Rioja region, together with other areas of the Ebro axis, in an urgent re-industrialization plan. Given the strategic situation of the Ebro axis as a link between the Atlantic Arc and the Mediterranean Arc, there is a justifiable need to bring back to La Rioja the industrial dynamism that has been lacking for so many years.
