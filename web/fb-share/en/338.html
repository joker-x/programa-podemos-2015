Liberalization of the AP-4 (Seville-Jerez)

The granting of this stretch of highway, after being extended a few years ago, is about to end. We will propose, given the lack of high-speed alternative roads, that the concession will not be extended and we will remove highway tolls. In this way, connection will be facilitated between the Campiña de Jerez and the river mouth of the Guadalquivir with Seville.
