Observatory of the right to a home

We will create an Observatory of the Right to a Home (ODV) with the aim of investigating and analyzing the housing situation in Spain.

The functions of the ODV will include the preparation of a periodic census of empty homes, the carrying out of a study of public policies, and the preparation of reports. The responsibilities of the ODV should not only be consultative but also ones of control, study, reporting, execution and preparation of legislative proposals.

The ODV will be made up of representatives of institutions and of civil society.
