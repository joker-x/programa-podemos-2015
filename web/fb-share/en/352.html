Promotion of a specific Balear model based on an economic and cooperative relationship with other territories of the Spanish State

  • We will adhere to existing regulation in the legal system that explicitly improves funding of Islas Baleares.
  • We will promote special territorial status Islas Baleares and the application of specific regulation within the European Union for such cases.
  • We will create a programme of state investment in Islas Baleares.
  • We will design a specific economic system for Islas Baleares that addresses inter-territorial compensation.
  • We will create an instrument of economical policies that grants objective validity and compensation to specific sectors, to guarantee the economic, labour and social development of our territory.
