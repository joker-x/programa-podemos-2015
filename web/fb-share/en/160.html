Children and adolescents: institutional priority

We will give greater visibility to childhood in institutions by means of the following measures:

Creation of a Permanent Commission of Infancy and Adolescence in Congress with the main function of assessing and following the impact of every legislative measure and public policy related to this demographic group.

Creation of the State Childhood and Adolescent Participation Council

Reform of the actual Childhood Observatory, with infant and adolescent representation; in order to improve its research competence of the social reality of the group and its influence in public policies. This observatory will ensure a greater visibility of childhood and adolescents in official statistics.
