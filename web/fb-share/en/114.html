Universal access to Health Care Card

We are going to carry out a legislative amendment aimed at safe-guarding the universal nature of the Spanish Health System. Thus we will ensure that our Health system works in accordance with Human Rights:

  • Universal Health Care coverage: right of access to a Health Care card for every person, including immigrants or emigrants with Spanish nationality, who lose access to universal health care coverage ninety days after leaving the country.
  • Repeal of the Royal Decree 16/2012, of 20th April, urgent measures to guarantee the sustainability of the Spanish Social Security system and to improve the quality and security of its services, by which the universality of healthcare in Spain was destroyed.
