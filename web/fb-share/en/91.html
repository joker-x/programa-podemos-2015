Gradual increase of the minimum wage up to 14 payments of 800 Euros per month by January 2018

We will gradually increase the minimum wage (SMI) to reach 800 Euros/month with 14 payments per year at the end of the first two year of the term of office, to ensure subsequent parity with 60% of the average wage established by the European Social Charter. Thus, at the end of the term of office that salary will be at least 950 Euros per month.
