Setting up of an audit of parliamentary debt

We will set up an audit of debt, and we will pay particular attention to the audit of the debt as a result of the bailout of the EU in order to shed light on the process of growth and internal restructuring of the debt that our country has experienced in recent years. The possibility of this audit is covered by European legislation itself: Regulation 472/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 21 May 2013, explicitly plans that 'A Member State subject to a macroeconomic adjustment programme shall carry out a comprehensive audit of its public finances in order, inter alia, to assess the reasons that led to the building up of excessive levels of debt as well as to track any possible irregularity'.
