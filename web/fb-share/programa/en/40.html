Investing in responsible science and R&D

We will bring public investment levels in R&D back to what they were before the economic crisis and will endeavour to return R&D investment targets in our country to the level of those in European countries (2% of GDP).

The objective will be to achieve an investment in R&D of 2.7% of the National Budget in 2017, and 3% in 2020.

We will ensure that loans from public funds given to R&D companies are used for what they were initially intended, and not as hidden subsidies. To this end, we will monitor and guarantee that funds intended to be invested in R&D are employed 100% in accordance with what was approved in the National Budget.
