Approval of an international legal framework for sovereign debt restructuring

We will defend the medium-term construction of a fairer, more effective and supportive global economic and financial system, representative of our multi-polar world.

To that end, the Spanish government will promote, from the multilateral organizations in which it participates, the adoption of an international legal framework for the restructuring of sovereign debt based on principles of responsible lending, in line with the UN Ad Hoc Committee.
