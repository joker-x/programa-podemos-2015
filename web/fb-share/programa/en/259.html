Repeal of the “gagging” law

We will immediately repeal the Statutory Law 4/2015, of 30th March, for the protection of citizen security, known as the “gagging” law, and we will establish new public safety legislation to facilitate the free exercise of fundamental rights of expression, assembly and demonstration. We will remove administrative sanctions, with the exception of unlawful acts whose gravity would make them subject to sanctions punishable under the Penal Code.
