Suspension of the Montoro Law

We will immediately repeal the Montoro Law (27/2013 Sustainability and Rationalization of Local Administration) because it endangers both minor entities and communal property and services.

We will promote a new legal framework to harmonize all three levels of government (local, territorial and national), and also to expand and update their responsibilities and budgets.

We will encourage a much-needed debate to reinforce local power, by and for the people.
