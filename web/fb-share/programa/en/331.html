New Plan for Africa

We will develop a new plan for Africa with the aim of supporting African societies in their efforts to promote democracy, human rights, gender equality and sustainable and inclusive development. This plan will be done in a framework of policy coherence, and will involve both Spanish civil society actors and African plaintiffs.

We will defend the strengthening of mechanisms to fight tax evasion and transparent control in natural resource management. In this sense, we will advocate a responsible Spanish business presence which is transparent and respectful with those principles.

Furthermore we will prioritize cooperation with African regional and sub-regional organizations in multilateral forums.
