Continued free rehabilitation and physiotherapy for people in situation of dependency or with functional diversities

Many of the people in situation of dependency or with some functional diversity require rehabilitation sessions or continued physiotherapy but cannot afford the expense. In many cases, they do not even have the right to them within the national health system. Given their therapeutic importance, we will suggest that these sessions (according to medical prescription) be included in the list of national health system services with the aim of guaranteeing their free and good quality universality.
