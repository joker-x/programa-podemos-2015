Strong and sustainable rural areas

We will guarantee the application of the current Regulation of Sustainable Development for the Rural Areas, from 13th December 2007, and the Royal Decree 752 / 2010 in order to reverse the tendency to depopulation and abandonment of the rural areas and also to encourage coordination between administrations.

We will develop a Comprehensive Participatory Plan by Regions (which until now has been paralysed by the Government) under the parameters of Social and Environmental Sustainability. We will create a new institutional framework which leads to the democratization of local administration and the recognition of self-government, which improves its responsibilities, with the objective of balancing the precarious institutional loyalty to the three existing levels of the government.
