Online research portal

In order to improve access to information about state-sponsored research, we will develop an online research website which will publish all project offers, collaboration agreements and calls to tender of all types, including contracts and jobs undertaken by public research institutions.

The portal will list all R&D public projects for tender in a biannual calendar, which must be complied with and agreed by the Government, the regions and other state-funded agencies.

It will also have an open access database that includes researchers' profiles, research groups, universities, PRIs and research projects at national level.

It will collect and make public all the statistical information contained in the Sistema Español de Ciencia, Technología e Innovación (SECTI): number of male and female students, budgets, projects, etc.
