Law for the free control over one’s life

We will pass a law that makes free control over one’s own life possible and, at the same time, this law will regulate associated decision-making. Likewise, we will repeal section 4 of the article 143 of the Penal Code.

We will promote a debate between citizens and health professional about levels of autonomy in a person’s decision-making during his or her last moments of life, their final wishes and a dignified death.
