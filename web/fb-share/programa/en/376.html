Revised draft of the underground railway in the south of Madrid

We will review the proposed tunneling of the railway in southern Madrid that splits the municipalities of Alcorcón, Fuenlabrada, Getafe, Leganés and Móstoles. To this end, we will analyze the cooperation agreement signed in 2011 between the Ministry of Development and the city councils of these municipalities, the viability of the commitments related to Ministry included in the agreement and, finally, possible alternatives and solutions to meet these commitments.
