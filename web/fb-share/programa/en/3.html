Stimulus and development of the green infrastructures

We will boost green infrastructures (collective transport networks, rail transport, water use efficiency, reforestation, etc.) and research, development and investigation plans (R&D) specially designed for green technology development, in particular that which encourages new methods of energy saving, new energy vectors and new modulation methods of generated electricity, such as hydrogen and solar thermoelectric, which allow the streamlining of renewable energies in the electric system.
