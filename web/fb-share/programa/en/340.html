Regulation of hydraulic exploitation of the basins of the Fardes, Genil and Guadiana Menor rivers

We will carry out the following proposals:

  • Repeal of water transfer projects of the Castril river.
  • Make an integral plan of reorganization and restoration of the river Genil between Granada and Loja.
  • Maintenance of the following reservoirs: El Portillo, San Clemente, Negratín, Francisco Abellan, Canales, Quéntar, Cubillas, Colomera and Bermejales.
