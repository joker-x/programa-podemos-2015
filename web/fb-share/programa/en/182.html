Funding of organisations within the third sector devoted to the assistance of people with functional diversity or dependency

We will create a social contract with the third sector to ensure the full exercise of the rights of people with functional diversity or in a dependent situation. By this means, the State will provide resources for the functioning of non-profit making organisations in the area of functional diversity and dependency as long as they assume an ethical commitment to continuous improvement, transparency and quality in the management of programmes and services, as well as mechanisms of democratic participation and governance that allow people with functional diversity or dependence to be treated as citizens with full right of participation in every aspect that affects them throughout their life.
