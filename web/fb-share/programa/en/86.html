Call for a European conference to discuss the coordinated debt restructuring of public debt within the Eurozone

We will propose a change in the expiration period of European outstanding debts through a bond exchange to enable old bonds to be replaced by new bonds which will be maintained indefinitely. The ECB would be the institution that should acquire, at nominal value, the old bonds and exchange them for new ones at 0% interest in order to keep them within balance indefinitely. The ECB should restructure the public debt of Eurozone economies when it is in excess of 60% of GDP.
