Programme of Supplementary Benefits

We will establish an extra benefit for those workers with low salaries in order to eliminate discrimination caused by the “poverty trap” and to assure that these workers reach sufficient levels of welfare. When people with this guaranteed benefit find a job or are able to work, this pension will be withdrawn gradually (not suddenly) in accordance with salary increases. The result will be to guarantee a minimum income for these workers of 900 Euros per month, which will be increased according to the number of members in the family. This complement will only be effective for those with a recognized salary of at least 250 Euros, in order to avoid possible fraud.
