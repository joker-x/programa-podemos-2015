Stable agricultural work

We will modify the Program of Development of Agricultural Employment (PFEA) in order to ensure stable and sustainable employment in rural areas in agricultural, agri-food and forestry projects and also in other sectors related to the conservation and maintenance of territory and of the natural, historical and landscape heritage of rural areas.

We will put an end to citizen dependency and patronage practices of town councils and entrepreneurs in the area of agriculture through the implementation of a Zero Poverty programme which will enable universalized rents and limit the requirement of signing up a daily work force. Thus, both the subsidy and agricultural incomes will be integrated into a perspective that will ensure a decent life for a particularly vulnerable group such as agricultural workers
