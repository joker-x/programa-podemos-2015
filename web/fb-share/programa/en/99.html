Increase of the involvement of workers in the management of their companies

We will promote a greater involvement of workers in the management of their companies with more than one hundred employees either by the strengthening of procedures of information and consultation established in the worker’s statute or by the implementation of a system similar to German supervisory boards. Furthermore, we will put forward a law, which will be voluntary for companies, to enable the financial involvement of workers. This law will enable the incorporation of some of the best experiences of workers’ involvement in other European countries.
