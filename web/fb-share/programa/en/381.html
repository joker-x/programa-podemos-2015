Regeneration of productive industrial areas

We will promote a recovery plan for the industrial areas of the left areas of Vizcaya (Barakaldo, Sestao, Portugalete and Santurtzi) and the industrial zone of Guipúzcoa (Errenteria and Pasaia) and will promote the production and development of new technologies in the metallurgical sector (increased R & D), products with high added value and training as a route towards the reintegration of professionals
