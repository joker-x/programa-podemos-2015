Regular audits of public entities

Regular audits of all public bodies and entities will be set in motion with the following goals:

  • Reviewing their statutes, so as to eliminate loopholes or duplication of public services.
  • Ensuring the independence of the public sector from the apparatus and interests of political parties.
  • Regaining those competencies and prerogatives that have been privatized or outsourced.
  • Reviewing assignments to instrumental entities so as to reverse those services that have been subcontracted, providing adequate budgeting and resources to better structure the public sector.
