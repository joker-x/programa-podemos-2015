Regulation Law of Sports Careers

In order to address one of the main requirements of this group, we will work in giving professional status to those positions and confer appropriate qualifications by developing a national law that unifies and sets a common basis in all regions for the legal regulation of sporting professions: classifying professional categories; specifying the duties and roles for each position and regulating the accreditation needed for qualifications, thereby regulating legal access to the occupation and combating intrusive access.
