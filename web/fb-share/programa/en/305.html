Emigration Office

We will create an Emigration Office with the following goals:

  • To centralize the organizations and services available, so that there is coordination between different programmes with the aim of helping emigrants or those who wish to emigrate.
  • To establish a communication line between different agents in Spain and abroad.

Furthermore, the Emigration Office will have headquarters in every Spanish province. These establishments will offer support, orientation and information on the country of destination so that emigrant may claim their labour and social rights and integration is encouraged.
